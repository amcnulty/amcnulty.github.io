About Us

The Contractor's Garage offers large usable industrial storage space in the heart of Johnson County Kansas designed exclusively for entrepreneurs and hobbyists including but not limited to landscapers, plumbers, electricians, specialty vehicles, or about anyone needing something larger than mini storage but smaller and less expensive than traditional incubator industrial space.

We currently have three locations and a fourth being built. All of our units have large drive-in doors and are very spacious. Unlike personal storage spaces, our units are built with the contractor in mind!

Developed, owned-in-part, and leased by:
Kevin Combs, CCIM - Commercial/Industrial Real Estate Broker and Developer.
